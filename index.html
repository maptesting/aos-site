Frequently asked questions

How does setup work? ⌄

We audit your call flow, import services/pricing and service area, connect your calendar/CRM, then deploy a tuned model. Most go live within ~72 hours.

Can it quote my exact pricing? ⌄

Yes. We ingest your menu, add-ons, deposits, travel rules, and any exceptions for accurate quotes.

What if someone asks for a human? ⌄

AOS routes to you or your team instantly, with the full transcript and context.

Do I need to change my phone system? ⌄

No. We can layer on top of your existing number or softphone. Keep what already works.
